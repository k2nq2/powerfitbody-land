Yes, the program is designed for all fitness levels. Beginners can start with basic exercises and progress as they get stronger. Advanced athletes can increase intensity and range of motion.

Each workout takes 20–40 minutes on average, depending on your fitness level and goals.

No, all exercises are bodyweight-based. You can train anywhere — at home, outdoors, or even while traveling.

Absolutely! The program includes exercises that boost metabolism, burn calories, and improve endurance.

Results depend on your starting point and commitment. Most participants see:
